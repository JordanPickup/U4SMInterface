U4SM Interface

Privacy Policy

Information Collection And Use

No personal information is colleced or stored by the U4SM Interface application. The provided connection information is stored only on your local computer. Your password is not stored anywhere, even on your local computer.

Transfer of Data

Any data stored in U4SM and accessed via the U4SM interface is not stored in the U4SM interface. It is transmitted only between U4SM and your computer.

Any transmitted data is not stored on, or transmitted through, any intermediary servers. It is only stored on your computer if you choose to export it to a file.

Disclosure of Data

As no data is collected, no data is available to disclose.

Security of Data

The security of your data is important to us, but remember that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. Accessing data via the U4SM Interface is subject to the same risks as accessing your data via the U4SM API.
